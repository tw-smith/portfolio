Analog EXIF


A simple web app built in Ruby on Rails to record EXIF style data when practising analog photograpy.


  • Tracks roll data like start and end dates, push level and development and scan statuses.

  • Records "EXIF style" data for individual photos such as aperture and shutter speed.

  • Under active development with features such as automated update of EXIF data of digital scan files in the pipeline.
